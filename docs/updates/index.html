In progress

Features currently under active development.

AI‑powered insights

Get intelligent feedback on your sessions with pattern recognition, strengths and weaknesses analysis, and personalized recommendations on what to practice next.

Mouse movement analysis

Automatic detection of overshoots, undershoots, and inefficient movements. See exactly how optimal your mouse control is and where to improve.

Cross‑platform support

Experimental builds for Linux and macOS with file watcher parity (mouse tracking remains Windows‑only for now).

Under consideration

Features we're exploring based on feedback and feasibility.

Encrypted cloud sync

Optional encrypted backups and sync for settings, favorites, and local analytics across devices.

Multi‑profile support

Separate profiles for multiple players on the same machine, each with their own stats and settings.

Mobile companion app

Read‑only view of your sessions, benchmarks, and trends on your phone for quick on‑the‑go review.

Aim Lab import

Import stats from other aim trainers to compare your progress across different platforms holistically.

Custom themes

Design and share your own color themes to make RefleK's match your setup perfectly.

Session sharing

Export a shareable session summary you can send to coaches or friends for review and feedback.
